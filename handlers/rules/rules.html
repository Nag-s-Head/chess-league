Our Rules

We run on a system of trust, and we hope that you respect this. We have a couple of rules to follow.

  1. Have Fun!
  2. Golden Rule: Don't be a dick. Be nice to people and use the self submit system responsibly.
  3. Play In The Pub: We want you to play in the pub (The Nag's Head) as that is where we are based.
  4. Time Control: We usually play with 20 minute, no increment, time control and advise you to do so as well. See FIDE's Laws of Chess for how timers work.
  5. Submit Accurately: We trust you to submit your games properly. If you submit games in error you can contact an admin who will fix it (see below).
